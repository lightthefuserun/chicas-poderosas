What is

Chicas Poderosas

Chicas Poderosas is a global community that ignites change by inspiring and developing women in media and creating opportunities for all voices to be heard.

Chicas Poderosas offers professional training to provide women in media with the concrete skills to take their media career to the next level, boost entrepreneurial projects and push forward female leadership in journalism.

Chicas Poderosas believes in a world of informed and equal societies where all voices are heard. We have an engaged and talented network of 5500 women across 18 countries creating content to transform the existing narrative ensuring that women-focused storytelling and editorial workflows affect how we report our world.

how we

Ignite change

Mediathons

A MediaThon is a hack-a-thon for media makers. Over two days, Chicas brings together a talented array of storytellers - from journalists and designers, to coders and technologists - to create and collaborate, developing innovative original content around a specific theme. Boundaries are challenged and networks are expanded. The objective is to disrupt the status quo, inspire new perspectives and promote media that educates on underrepresented issues, changing the narrative and making new headlines.

Know more

Powerful investigations

Power reports (investigaciones poderosas) are deep dive, collaborative special reports involving Chicas journalists from multiple countries that face a similar challenge. Power reports deliver high-quality journalism products on under-represented issues with a regional impact.

Know more

New Ventures Lab

The New Ventures Lab (NVL) is a 20-week accelerator for independent, women-led media outlets in Latin America. The NVL provides mentorship and training for female media entrepreneurs to build sustainable business models while developing their leadership skills. Previous NVL teams are transforming the Latin American media landscape, generating positive social impact and creating new female-driven leadership models.
